About Us

Hello! We are the Ellis Family. Caden grew up in Abilene, Texas and went on a mission to Buenos Aires, Argentina from 2016-2018. Courtney was raised in Pleasant Grove, Utah. We met each other at BYU in the fall of 2018 during both of our first semesters at BYU. Eventually we started dating in the spring of 2019. After a busy summer of traveling and getting to know each other more (mostly through long distance) we got engaged in August and married on November 23, 2019.

We love to play games and watch shows together! If you ever want to play games with us just ask! We also enjoy making good food together and going to Disneyland. We love both the Marvel and Star Wars universes. Caden has enjoyed introducing Courtney to these, and more, during their relationship.

Caden studied Computer Engineering at BYU and loves to learn new things. He is currently working at SchedMD in Lehi, Utah developing the Slurm workload manager.

Courtney is a fine specimen to behold. She is breath taking and frankly Caden is lucky she even gave him a chance. She loves ice cream. She is currently working at Eide Bailly as a Salesforce Developer and Consultant.
